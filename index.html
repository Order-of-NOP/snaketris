SnaketriS

A co-op game for 2 players!

Press ENTER to start

Snake (Player 1) eats fruit to grow

When a fruit reaches the floor, it roots and becomes unremovable (but still counts when a line scores)

Don't hit things with your head, Snake, unless you want to fall apart into pieces

Press ENTER to continue

Hitting Snake with Tetrimino (Player 2) cuts it's tail

Player 1, use cursor keys to control Snake

Player 2, you control Tetrimino with WASD.

Your aim is to score as many points as you can. Have fun!
